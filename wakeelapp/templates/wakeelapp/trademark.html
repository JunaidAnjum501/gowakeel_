What is a Trademark?

A trademark is any distinctive sign that identifies your business, products, or services in the marketplace. It could be a:

  • Business name
  • Logo
  • Slogan
  • Symbol or design
  • Unique packaging style

Once registered, your trademark at IPO (Intellectual property organization of Pakistan) it becomes your exclusive legal property, giving you the right to take action against anyone who tries to copy or misuse it.
