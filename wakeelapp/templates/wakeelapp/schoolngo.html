NGO Registration Services

We offer full consultancy for NGO, NPO, Trust, Welfare Society, or Charity organization registration under one of the following frameworks:

Registration Frameworks

  • Societies Registration Act, 1860

    Ideal for educational, scientific, and charitable organizations with 7+ members.

  • Voluntary Social Welfare Agencies Ordinance, 1961

    Suitable for organizations focused on social welfare activities.

  • Trust Act, 1882

    Appropriate for managing assets for charitable purposes with trustees.

  • Section 42 of Companies Act, 2017 (SECP)

    For larger organizations seeking a corporate structure with limited liability.

Documents Required for Registration

The following documents are typically required for school and NGO registration in Pakistan. Our team will guide you through gathering these documents and ensure they meet all legal requirements.

For School or Educational Society

Personal Identification

  • CNIC copies of founder(s)
  • Passport-size photographs

Property Documentation

  • Proof of address (school premises or rented space)
  • Lease agreement or property ownership documents

Educational Documentation

  • Proposed school name and structure
  • Educational qualifications (optional)
  • Curriculum/plan of education
  • Memorandum and rules (we will help draft them)

For NGO/Trust/Society

Member Documentation

  • CNICs of at least 3 members/trustees
  • Passport-size photographs

Organization Documentation

  • Office address proof
  • Objectives and mission of the organization
  • Name reservation letter (we handle this)

Legal Documentation

  • Draft Constitution or Trust Deed
  • Bank account details (after approval)
