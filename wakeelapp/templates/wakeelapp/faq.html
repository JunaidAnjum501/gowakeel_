To begin the company registration process:

  1. Visit GoWakeel's Business Services section
  2. Review our comprehensive guides on company registration
  3. Choose the service package that fits your needs
  4. Submit the required information through our secure portal
  5. Our experts will guide you through the rest of the process

The duration for company registration varies based on several factors:

  • Type of company structure (Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, Private Limited)
  • Completeness of documentation provided
  • Current processing times at government departments

Typically, the process takes between 7-21 working days. For precise timelines based on your specific requirements, please contact our customer support team.
