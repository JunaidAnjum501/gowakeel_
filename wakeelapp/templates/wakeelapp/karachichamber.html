Required Documents

To expedite your Karachi Chamber of Commerce registration, please have the following documents ready:

  • Copy of CNIC

    Valid computerized national identity card of the business owner/directors

  • Contact Information

    Mobile number and email address for official communication

  • Bank Maintenance Certificate

    Recent bank maintenance certificate from your business account

  • NTN Certificate and Returns

    Copy of National Tax Number certificate and submitted tax returns

  • Sales Tax Certificate (if available)

    Copy of sales tax registration certificate (not mandatory)

  • Business Address

    Address must match the one registered with NTN

  • Import/Export Items List

    Detailed list of items you import or export

  • Two Proposers

    Two proposers who are existing members of the Karachi Chamber of Commerce

  • Pay Order

    Pay order of PKR 10,000/- in favor of Karachi Chamber of Commerce

  • Photograph

    1 passport size photograph with white background

Important Note

All documents must be clear, legible copies. Having these documents ready before starting the application will significantly speed up the registration process.
