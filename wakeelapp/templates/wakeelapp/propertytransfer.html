Property mutation is the process of transferring a property's title from one person to another in the revenue records. It's important because:

  • It establishes your legal ownership in government records
  • It's required for paying property taxes in your name
  • It protects you from fraudulent claims on your property
  • It's necessary for obtaining utility connections
  • It's essential for any future property transactions

The property transfer timeline varies depending on the authority and property type:

  • DHA: Typically 30-45 days
  • Cantonment Board: Approximately 45-60 days
  • Military Estate (MEO): Around 60-90 days

The basic documents required for property transfer include:
