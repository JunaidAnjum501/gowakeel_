Pakistan Software Export Board (PSEB) registration is essential for IT and ITeS companies in Pakistan that aim to operate professionally, expand globally, and benefit from government incentives. At Go Wakeel, we provide end-to-end support for PSEB registration, making it fast, smooth, and easy.

Benefits of PSEB Registration

With PSEB registration, your company becomes eligible for:

Government Incentives

Access to government incentives and grants designed specifically for the IT sector to boost growth and innovation.

Export Certification

Official export certification for IT services, enhancing your credibility with international clients.

International Exposure

Participation in international exhibitions and delegations to showcase your services globally.

Visa Facilitation

Access to visa facilitation letters, making international business travel easier for you and your team.

Office Space

Priority access to office space in IT parks and STPs (Software Technology Parks) at competitive rates.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with other IT companies, potential clients, and investors through PSEB-organized events and platforms.

Who Should Register with PSEB?

You must register if you are:

Freelancers & Independent Contractors

If you're a freelancer or independent contractor offering IT services such as:

  • Web development
  • Mobile app development
  • Software engineering
  • UI/UX design
  • Digital marketing services

Software Houses & IT Firms

If you operate a software house or IT consultancy firm providing services like:

  • Custom software development
  • Enterprise solutions
  • IT consulting
  • System integration
  • Cloud services

BPO Service Providers

If you're a Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) service provider offering:

  • Call center services
  • Back-office operations
  • Data processing
  • Customer support
  • Technical support

Startups & Digital Service Exporters

If you're a startup looking to expand operations or an exporter of digital services such as:

  • Web development
  • App development
  • SEO and digital marketing
  • Graphic design
  • Content creation

Documents Required for PSEB Registration

To apply for PSEB registration, you'll need to prepare the following documents:

Personal Identification

  • Valid CNIC of the business owner or CEO

Tax Documentation

  • NTN (National Tax Number)
  • Tax return or financials (last year, if available)

Business Documentation

  • SECP Certificate or Business Registration (sole proprietorship/partnership)
  • Company profile or website link
  • List of employees (if applicable)

Office Documentation

  • Office lease agreement or ownership proof
